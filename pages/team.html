The Team

Sohini Shah



Sohini is a rising senior at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science. She is the Founder and Chief Planner at SteerMyEvents. She has been passionate about organizing events since she was a little kid. Always one to want to celebrate her birthday in the best possible manner since the age of 6, Sohini, a Massachusetts local, started SteerMyEvent as a 14 year-old, with the objective to change the way people approach their events. Her goal is to make the process of organizing events as pocket-friendly, stress-free, and smooth as possible, and so far, SteerMyEvent has been able to more than live up to these principles and goals.
